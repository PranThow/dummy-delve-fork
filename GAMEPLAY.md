# Dummy Delve - Gameplay Guide

## Overview

Dummy Delve is a turn-based dungeon crawler where you must defeat the Goblin King by progressing through procedurally generated rooms filled with goblin enemies.

## Main Game Loop

### 1. Menu Screen
- Start the game with "START RUN"
- This generates a new Age of Goblins run

### 2. Room Exploration
- View your current room number (e.g., "Room 1 / 10")
- See the enemy type you'll face
- Press "BEGIN" to start combat

### 3. Combat
Combat is turn-based and card-based:

#### Your Turn
- Click on cards in your hand to play them
- Each card costs energy (shown in the card corner)
- You have 3 energy per turn
- Cards automatically go to the discard pile after playing
- When you've done your actions, press "END TURN"

#### Enemy Turn
- The enemy automatically attacks for damage
- Damage is reduced by your defense
- Defense resets after each turn

#### Card Types

**Attack Cards** - Deal damage to the enemy
- Slash: 1 energy, 5 damage
- Heavy Strike: 2 energy, 10 damage
- Double Strike: 2 energy, 3+3 damage

**Skill Cards** - Provide defensive or utility
- Guard: 1 energy, gain 5 defense
- Fortify: 2 energy, gain 10 defense
- Regenerate: 2 energy, heal 8 HP

**Item Cards** - Usually free utility
- Bread: 0 energy, heal 3 HP
- Antidote: 1 energy, heal 5 HP

### 4. Combat Victory
- When an enemy is defeated, you see a "NEXT" button
- This takes you to the reward screen

### 5. Reward Selection
After combat (except after boss), choose 1 of 3 rewards:
- **New Card**: Adds a card to your deck
- **Gold**: Currency (currently cosmetic)
- **Health**: Restores HP

### 6. Continue or Victory
- Progress through rooms until you reach the boss (Room 10)
- Defeat the Goblin King to win the run
- Return to menu to start a new run

## Progression

### Rooms
- **Combat Rooms (1-9)**: Face various goblins
  - Goblin: Basic enemy
  - Goblin Archer: Stronger attack
  - Goblin Chief: Much stronger
- **Boss Room (10)**: Face the Goblin King (final boss)

### Difficulty
Each enemy type has different stats:
- Different HP values
- Different attack power
- Various combinations in procedurally generated runs

## Strategy Tips

1. **Manage Your Energy**: You have 3 energy per turn, plan your cards carefully
2. **Defense is Key**: Use defense cards to reduce incoming damage
3. **Deck Building**: As you progress, you'll acquire new cards
4. **Health Management**: Keep an eye on your HP and heal when needed
5. **Discard Pile**: When your deck runs out, discard pile is shuffled back

## Stats Reference

### Player
- Max HP: 50
- Energy per turn: 3
- Starting defense: 0 (resets each turn)

### Enemies
- **Goblin**: 15 HP, 3 attack
- **Goblin Archer**: 12 HP, 5 attack
- **Goblin Chief**: 25 HP, 7 attack
- **Goblin King**: 50 HP, 10 attack

## Winning Strategies

1. **Defensive Playstyle**: Use guard/fortify to minimize damage
2. **Aggressive Playstyle**: Play offensive cards to end combat quickly
3. **Balanced Playstyle**: Mix damage and defense based on enemy type
4. **Health Management**: Save healing cards for when you need them most

## Controls

- **Click Card**: Play a card from your hand (must have enough energy)
- **End Turn**: Complete your turn and let the enemy attack
- **Next**: After combat, proceed to rewards
- **Button**: Select menu options and rewards

Enjoy your adventure in Dummy Delve!
